Fried potatoes

Are You Ready to Sizzle with the Crispiest Fried Potatoes?

"Welcome to our crispy potato haven! Experience the irresistible crunch and mouthwatering flavor that will leave you longing for more.

Get ready for fried potato bliss!"

Ingredients

  • 3 medium russet potatoes, peeled
  • 1 large egg, beaten, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon grated onion
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or more to taste
  • ¼ cup peanut oil for frying, or as needed

Steps

  1. Slice peeled potatoes and run through a food processor fitted with the grater attachment.
  2. Place shredded potatoes in a cheesecloth and wring, extracting as much moisture as possible.
  3. Measure 2 cups shredded, drained potatoes and place into a medium bowl; save any extra for another use.
  4. Add egg, flour, onion, and salt, and stir until well combined.
  5. Heat 1/4 inch of oil in a large, heavy-bottomed skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. Place large spoonfuls of potato mixture into the hot oil, pressing down on them to form 1/4- to 1/2-inch-thick patties.
  7. Cook until golden brown, 4 to 5 minutes. Flip and repeat on the other side.
  8. Remove to a paper towel-lined plate to drain while you cook any remaining latkes.
  9. Serve hot.
